## About

I am an epidemiology researcher and international medical graduate dedicated to preventive medicine and the use of epidemiological methods to reduce the burden of chronic diseases. My work focuses on **cardiovascular, cancer, and chronic disease epidemiology**, clinical trial methodology, and large national databases and biomarker research to identify actionable targets for disease prevention.
