I am an international medical graduate and epidemiology researcher with a background spanning clinical medicine in India and graduate public health training at Emory University's Rollins School of Public Health.

My work focuses on **cardiovascular, nutritional, and chronic disease epidemiology**, evidence synthesis methodology, and the interrogation of large national databases — including NHANES and PREDIMED-PLUS — to identify actionable biomarkers and lifestyle targets for disease prevention.

Currently based at the **Smidt Heart Institute, Cedars-Sinai Medical Center** in Los Angeles, I am actively building an independent research portfolio at the intersection of nutrition, cardiometabolic risk, and evidence synthesis.
