About

Researcher. Clinician.
Evidence synthesist.

I am an international medical graduate and epidemiology researcher with a background spanning clinical medicine in India and graduate public health training at Emory University's Rollins School of Public Health.


My work focuses on cardiovascular, cancer, and chronic disease epidemiology, evidence synthesis methodology, and the interrogation of large national databases — NHANES, PREDIMED-PLUS, and beyond — to identify actionable biomarkers and lifestyle targets for disease prevention.


Currently based at the Smidt Heart Institute, Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles, I am actively building an independent research portfolio in nutritional and cardiovascular epidemiology.
